# Community Meetings

Butler community meetings are held bi-weekly to discuss development, roadmap, and community topics.

## Schedule

**When**: Every other Thursday at 10:00 AM Central Time (US)

**Where**: Video call link posted in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/butlerdotdev/butler/discussions) before each meeting

**Calendar**: [Add to Google Calendar](#) *(coming soon)*

## Agenda

Typical agenda:

1. Welcome and introductions (5 min)
2. Release updates (10 min)
3. Current work discussion (20 min)
4. Community topics and Q&A (15 min)
5. Action items and next steps (10 min)

### Proposing Agenda Items

Add agenda items by commenting on the meeting issue in Discussions before the meeting.

## First-Time Attendees

Welcome! Here's how to get the most from meetings:

1. Review the agenda beforehand
2. Join a few minutes early
3. Introduce yourself
4. Feel free to ask questions
5. Mute when not speaking
